# Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist for College Students in India

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone, even if they sound genuine
- Before trusting any call or message, take a moment to check who it’s really from
- Always download payment apps from official app stores so you don’t end up using fake ones
- Try not to click on random or unknown links, as they can be risky
- Turn on extra security options like two-factor authentication to stay safer
- Keep checking your account or app regularly so you notice anything unusual quickly
- Before sending money, just pause and double-check everything carefully
- Make sure your phone is always updated so it stays secure and works properly
