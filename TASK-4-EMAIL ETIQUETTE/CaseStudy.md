# Part A - Case study: UPI/Online payment fraud

UPI fraud is basically when someone fools you into sending them money through apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm. These scams are becoming very common now because almost everyone uses online payments every day.

Most of the time, it starts with a call or a message. The person might act like they are from your bank or customer care. Sometimes they scare you by saying there’s a problem with your account, and other times they try to excite you by saying you’ve won some prize or cashback.

Once you start believing them, they slowly ask for important details like your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank info. In some cases, they send a payment request or a link that looks completely real. If you accept it or share your details without thinking much, the money gets deducted from your account.

Students often become easy targets because they use these apps a lot and may act quickly without double-checking.

Losing money like this can be really stressful and frustrating. On top of that, reporting the issue or getting the money back is not always easy.
